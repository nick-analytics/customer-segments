What to do with this

Strategic Recommendations

Three actions the business can take immediately, based directly on what the data reveals.

Recommendation 01
Launch a High Value retention programme
With 45% of revenue concentrated in just 6% of customers, the risk of churn in this segment is disproportionately high. A dedicated retention programme - personalised outreach, exclusive previews, priority service, directly protects the business's most critical revenue stream.
Who acts on this →
Marketing & CRM team
Recommendation 02
Test a basket-size upsell campaign for Steady Regulars
Steady Regulars already trust the brand and return consistently. A targeted upsell or bundle offer could lift average spend from $339 without significant marketing investment. Even modest gains across 40% of the base add up quickly.
Who acts on this →
Marketing & Merchandising
Recommendation 03
Run a win-back campaign for Dormant Buyers
53% of customers are currently inactive. A segmented win-back sequence starting with a low-friction re-engagement offer could recover a portion of this group at far lower cost than acquiring new customers.
Who acts on this →
Email & Retention Marketing

About this analysis

Methodology

A brief overview of the data, approach, and tools used to produce this segmentation.

Data Source
Retail transaction records: 5,799 customers across a defined study period
Approach
RFM analysis (Recency, Frequency, Monetary value) used to score and cluster customers into behavioural segments
Segmentation Method
K-means and DBScan clustering applied to normalised RFM scores to identify 4 distinct customer groups
Tools Used
KNIME, Python (pandas, scikit-learn), data visualisation with Adobe suite, HTML/CSS for delivery
